# Product Vision — AI Growth OS

**Version:** 1.0
**Date:** 2026-03-28

---

## What Is This?

AI Growth OS is a **local, self-hosted, open-source intelligence platform** that helps companies understand their audience, discover growth opportunities, and improve their discoverability — without relying on any external AI provider.

A company connects their website and sources of truth. The platform runs continuously, discovering trends, understanding the brand, matching the right audiences, and surfacing actionable growth recommendations.

Everything runs on the company's own infrastructure. No data leaves the deployment.

---

## The Core Problem We Solve

Growth and marketing teams face three hard problems:

1. **Signal overload** — thousands of trends, communities, and signals. Hard to know which are relevant.
2. **Context gap** — generic AI tools don't understand your product, audience, or positioning.
3. **AI discoverability gap** — your content may not be optimized for how AI search engines discover and cite content.

Traditional tools require subscription costs, data sharing, and proprietary lock-in.

**AI Growth OS solves this locally.**

## Core Use Cases

### Use Case 1: Brand Intelligence
> "What do we actually know about our product, audience, and positioning?"

- Connect your website
- The platform crawls it and builds a structured brand profile
- Extracts: value proposition, ICP, features, pricing signals, competitive positioning
- Shows gaps and recommendations

### Use Case 2: Trend Discovery
> "What are people talking about that's relevant to us?"

- Connect Reddit communities, RSS feeds, news sources
- Platform detects rising topics, pain points, competitor mentions
- Scores each trend's relevance to your brand
- Surfaces opportunities: "This trend matches your product positioning"

### Use Case 3: GEO / AI Discoverability
> "Are we visible to AI search engines like Perplexity, ChatGPT, and Gemini?"

- Analyzes your site for AI crawler access
- Checks structured data, content citability, llms.txt
- Scores AI discoverability with an overall GEO score
- Generates specific improvement recommendations

### Use Case 4: Audience Matching
> "Who specifically should we be targeting?"

- From trend data and site analysis, identify relevant communities and segments
- Score each segment by purchase intent and brand fit
- Reveal where your audience actually hangs out

### Use Case 5: Growth Recommendations
> "What specific actions should we take this week?"

- Combines all intelligence into a prioritized opportunity feed
- Each recommendation has evidence, confidence, and expected impact
- Human approval required before any automation

After using AI Growth OS for 30 days, a user should be able to:

1. Know their brand's value proposition, ICP, and content gaps better than before
2. Have a feed of relevant, scored trends updated continuously from their sources
3. Have a GEO score with specific, actionable improvements
